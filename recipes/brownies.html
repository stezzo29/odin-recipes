The BEST Brownie Recipe!


Description

This is the BEST brownie recipe you've ever tried! They're the perfect mix of gooey, chocolatey, and crispy on the edges.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs + water
  • Powdered sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1.  First, mix together the dry and wet ingredients in two separate bowls.
  2.  Next, combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  3.  Then, pour the batter into an 8Ã—8 inch baking pan lined with parchment paper.
  4.  Bake at 325 degrees for 40-45 minutes.
  5.  Enjoy!
